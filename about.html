More About Us

Our Teachers

Mr. Chiu

Joyful House was founded in 2003 by Mr. Caesar Chiu, who realized that learning instruments can be quite stressful due to a multitude of reasons, and wanted to foster a safe, comfortable learning environment for students.

Mr. Chiu realized his passion for the arts at a very young age, but was forced to study under quite a challenging environment.

Thankfully, he never lost his flame and wants to pass on the torch to others who wish to play modern instruments.

He has graduated from the Berklee College of Music located in the United States and has received multiple awards for his performances.

Under his tutelage, his students will certainly be able to perform at top levels.

Mr. Lok

A close and dear friend of Mr. Chiu, Mr. Lok teaches both guitars and bass, and his skill will certainly show itself.

Surpassing Grade 8 and above in all 3 instruments, he is more than capable of teaching any student and overcoming any challenge.

His stage presence and charisma have allowed him to perform in many concerts across Hong Kong, and is the guitarist of a band called "The Croks".

If you aspire to be in a band, there is no one better to teach you than Mr. Lok.

Ms. Lok

Proficient both in the drums and in teaching, Ms. Lok is one of the best teachers you will ever meet.

Working in the Joyful House Playgroup before transferring here, she is the most experienced with kids and her teaching methods.

Studying overseas at the Berklee College of Music alongside Mr. Chiu, she has received her diploma in drumming and is working to pass her talents on to others.
