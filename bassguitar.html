BASS GUITAR

Just because it seems similar to the electric guitar does not mean that they are identical -
the bass guitar provides rhythm and support for the electric guitar.

With one of the lowest pitch ranges and only 4 strings,
it is no wonder that this instrument is able to provide the rhythm so effectively.

However, there are multiple styles of picking, and rhythm can be harder to grasp than you think.
Through our teaching, you will be able to master this in no time.

Teachers: Mr. Chiu, Mr. Lok
